Crystals Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on the crystals below you will add points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If you go over the random number, you will lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until they are clicked. When the game restarts, the value of each crystal will change as well.
